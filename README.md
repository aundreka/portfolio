# Piano Themed Portfolio

This site is my personal portfolio, crafted over several months of experimentation and refinement to showcase the work I care about most. Every page and interaction reflects long evenings spent polishing content, layouts, and subtle animations so it feels like a living record of my journey.

## Why the Piano Theme

Music has always been my favorite hobby, and the piano is the instrument I keep coming back to. I borrowed its rhythm, warmth, and layering from the instrument’s feel: the colors, gradients, and spacing mimic a stage-lit piano outline, and the gentle transitions are meant to echo the rise and fall of a melody. The goal is to invite visitors into a space that feels both personal and performative—like listening to my compositions while hearing a few stories in between.

## What’s Here

- **Projects** – A rotating list of recent work, with notes about what I learned on each project so the story behind the code is easy to follow.
- **About & Values** – A short biography plus the principles I follow when approaching new challenges.
- **Media & Sounds** – Pieces of the piano-inspired world: samples, video clips, or sketches baked right into the layout.

## Built With

- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript for lightweight performance
- Custom CSS gradients and keyframe-based transitions to keep the piano mood alive
- Responsive design so the experience feels good on both desktop and tablet displays

## Run Locally

1. Open `index.html` in a browser or serve the directory from any static host.
2. Make edits in `css/` or `js/` to update styling and interactions.

If you'd like to focus on a particular section or want help reworking the theme, I’m happy to collaborate.
